RAKTDAAN means life-sustaining significance, emphasizing the crucial role of giving blood for sustaining life and vitality. It refers to the voluntary act of giving one's blood for medical purposes, such as transfusions for patients in need.



Functions Of RAKTDAAN:

Blood Donation:

Offer guidance on the importance of blood donation, eligibility criteria, and the donation process. Provide details on where and how to donate blood, including blood donation centers or drives.

FAQ Section:

Answer common questions people have about blood, donation, transfusions, disorders, and any other related topics.

Locator Map:

Provide a searchable map feature or a list of blood donation centers, hospitals, or upcoming blood drives where visitors can donate.

Appointment Scheduling:

Offer an option to schedule appointments for blood donation at partnering centers or upcoming drives.
